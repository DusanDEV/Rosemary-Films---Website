Rosemary Films

Name Rosemary Films came to my mind spontaneously, I was thinking how to present my creative side of the brain to people, and it was Rosemary who made it possible for me when I saw it at several weddings. Rosemary will be to me as a Batman a bat, my trademark and all my creativity, emotion and love.


Going out in front of the altar, holding hands, shaking hands with emotion, tears of parental joy, the excitement and screaming of friends, and the happy prayer of priests all best show one part of that most beautiful day. I may be biased and subjective, but the film best show it all. Wedding film is supposed to be the return of all those emotions that day. When i see you, in my head I immediately know what the movie will be like and that movie will one day pick up the award, evan if it is a moral one.

LATEST FILMS

I may be slightly biased, but for me a wedding film should be the most immersive and transportive way of reliving a wedding day. All the idiosyncrasies of the wedding that embody the individuality and mannerisms of the couple and their closest people; the last-minute quickening of nerves, the warm greetings of family and friends who haven’t met in years, the subtle looks between a couple and the quirks and character of the setting, can be captured and relived through film. A well-made wedding film can plunge you back into those moments, paired with the sounds, voices and words of the day.
