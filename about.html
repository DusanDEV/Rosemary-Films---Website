Hey!


I'm Nemanja, a visual storyteller, filmmaker (videographer) and photographer. My destination is wherever you are but I call my home in Belgrade where I live with my wife. My biggest interest in the world of film and videography is attracted to weddings. What I love about weddings are emotions that can trigger, come to life and rock. The emotions of the couple, their loved ones, the heartfelt greetings of family and friends who have not met in years, the subtle looks of a loving couple, the sparkles in their eyes, and the character of an environment that can be captured and relived through film. I believe that weddings are something special, grand, a reminder of the best in a person, the embodiment of love, joy, beauty and togetherness. For me, film is not just a camera shoot, I care and pay attention to the small details and blend the narrative that really tells your story. That doesn't mean I approach each couple the same way, quite the contrary.

Carefully using natural lighting, composition and equipment, I absorb the atmosphere of wedding, love, customs, body language, glances, words, tears, laughter, joy, which can all be relived on film. Apart from filming in Belgrade, I am not stranger to destination filming, which gives me great pleasure. I am given the opportunity to express through the film the unique culture, locations and heritage of a particular area, city or state. I like to meet new, creative and cheerful people. Then I find out that we all have a different journey through life and that each trip is exciting. In the beginning strangers, we will become friends. What happens on your wedding day becomes eternal, for you, for me, for us. This is why you are special to me and that is why I make films!
